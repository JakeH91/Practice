About Me

Over the past few years I have been lucky enough to see new parts of the world, and experience a variety of cultures. This has truly affected my view of the world, and of people.

During my travels, I often experienced the true nature of hospitality and kindness, frequently through online communities such as Couchsurfing and HelpX.

Through these experiences, I decided to dedicate my time to the study of Web Development. It is my goal to create sites with similar ambitions to those mentioned above. I began these studies in September of 2015, and have been progressing at a steady pace since then, mainly through tools such as Free Code Camp and The Odin Project.
